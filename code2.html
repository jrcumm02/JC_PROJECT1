Welcome to the World of John Raymond. . .

  • Since the age of 3 I have been attracted to the ideas of design, I first picked up pencils and drew on pieces of paper. My father soon saw fit to provide me with Magna doodle, and Etch-a-scetch. from there I was hooked.

  • Next I evolved to a V-tech Socrates. using color and shapes, which pushed into a Apple Macintosh Performa 575. Next a TI book graced my life, and along the way I developed a passion for making and creating beautiful things.

  • I pursued Fine Arts and Design at the University of Louisville. During that time I confirmed my desire to follow the path of architecture. I Pushed my way into the graduate Program at the University of Cincinnati.

  • After being refreshed with new ideas and artistic freedom, I then Returned to Louisville Ky, where I resumed Class work at Indiana University Southeast. Where I am now Finishing My Masters thesis for the Interdisciplinary Studies Program.
